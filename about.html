OUR STORY

Welcome to Hills Castle Inn Hotel

Set against the serene and scenic backdrop of surrounding hills and open mountain views, Hills Castle Inn Hotel offers a peaceful retreat combined with dependable modern hospitality in Bihar. We proudly serve tourists, Buddhist pilgrims, families, wedding guests, and business travelers from India and abroad, with strict hygiene and safety practices and support for international guests and families.
