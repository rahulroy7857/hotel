B) Corporate & Group Trust Builders

  • Meeting setup (classroom + boardroom styles) (2–3 photos)

C) Cleanliness & Safety Proof (silent “yes” moments)

  • Fresh linen / made bed close-up (1 photo)
  • Well lit corridor / common area (1 photo)
  • Staff welcoming shot (1 photo) (human warmth increases conversion)
  • Parking / drop-off zone (1 photo) (helps families & foreign guests)

D) Photo Style Rules (to increase bookings)

  • Wide angle, straight lines, bright natural light

  • Add captions + ALT on upload (don’t skip — it improves SEO + accessibility)
